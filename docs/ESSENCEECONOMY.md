# ESSENCEECONOMY — Essence Drop & Shop Balance

*Last updated April 16, 2026*

---

## Overview

Essence 🟠 is the shop currency dropped by enemies. It is separate from XP. Essence funds item purchases at the hub shop. Balance is tuned so a full zone clear gives enough Essence for 1–2 meaningful purchases.

---

## Enemy Drop Values

| Enemy | Essence Drop | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Grunt | 5–15 | Flat random in range |
| Ranged | 10–20 | Slightly higher — harder to reach |
| Bruiser | 25–40 | High value, high effort |
| Gate Warden (miniboss) | 80–120 | Guaranteed on kill |
| VRAEL | 200–300 | Boss clear bonus |
| ZARTH | 250–350 | Boss clear bonus |
| KIBAD | 275–375 | Boss clear bonus |
| THYXIS | 300–400 | Boss clear bonus |

Drops spawn as a collectible on the ground at enemy death position. Auto-collected when player walks over within 40px. No time limit on collection.

## Shop Rules

- Shop shows **5 items** per visit (random from pool, weighted by upgrade path after L7).
- Max **2 purchases** per visit.
- **Reroll cost:** 30 Essence. Unlimited rerolls.
- Spell-specific items only appear **after Level 3** (Advanced spell unlocked).
- Path-weighted items appear **after Level 7** (path locked).
- Items do not carry over between runs.

---

## Wipe / Death Economy

- On full party wipe: run ends, all XP and Essence lost.
- **No between-run persistence** in MVP — each run starts fresh.
- Post-MVP: retain 30% Essence as a soft currency for permanent upgrades.

---

## Balance Levers

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Shop feels too cheap | Increase item costs by 20%, reduce drop rates by 10% |
| Players can't afford anything | Reduce item costs by 15%, increase bruiser drops by 10 |
| Boss Essence too low | Increase boss drop floor by 50 |
| Too much Essence late-game | Cap total carry at 2000, excess converts to XP at 1:5 ratio |
