# HUNTIX — Progression & Shop

> *Levels give you tools. The shop lets you sharpen them.*

*Last updated April 15, 2026*

---

## Overview

Huntix has two interlinked progression systems that run in parallel every run:

| System | Currency | Resets on wipe? | Feel |
|--------|----------|-----------------|------|
| **Levelling** | XP ⚡ (auto, earned by playing well) | Full reset | Growth — you become your hunter |
| **Shop** | Essence 🟠 (dropped by enemies) | Lose 50%, keep 50% | Economy — you decide what to sharpen |

They are designed to feed each other. Levels unlock spells. The shop has items that amplify those spells. By the end of a clean run you have a fully realised hunter build with a playstyle that is distinctly yours.

---

## Part 1 — Levelling

### 10 Levels Per Run

A run through 4 zones fighting ~80+ enemies at an aggressive pace generates ~9,000 XP. A clean skilled run hits level 10 on the final boss kill — climactic and earned. A messier run hits L8–9 (Ultimate still unlocked, one capstone short).

### XP Sources

XP rewards **skill and aggression**, not just killing.

| Action | XP |
|--------|----|
| Light kill | 10 |
| Heavy / finisher kill | 25 |
| Combo ×5 bonus | +20 |
| Combo ×10 bonus | +50 |
| Dodge cancel into attack | +5 |
| Status effect proc (per tick) | +8 |
| Boss kill | 500 |
| Zone clear (all waves) | 150 |
| No-damage wave clear bonus | +100 |

### Level Table

| Level | XP to reach (cumulative) | What happens |
|-------|--------------------------|----------------|
| **1** | — | Start with Minor spell + base combo |
| **2** | 300 | 🃏 Pick 1 of 3 stat cards (damage / speed / HP) |
| **3** | 700 | 🔓 **Advanced spell unlocked** |
| **4** | 1,300 | 🃏 Pick 1 of 3 Minor spell modifications |
| **5** | 2,000 | 🃏 Pick 1 of 3 stat cards OR passive ability |
| **6** | 3,000 | 🃏 Pick 1 of 3 Advanced spell modifications |
| **7** | 4,200 | 🔒 **Lock upgrade path** — Power / Survival / Mobility / Style |
| **8** | 5,600 | 🃏 3 path-specific upgrade cards |
| **9** | 7,200 | 🔓 **Ultimate spell unlocked** |
| **10** | 9,000 | 🃏 Path **capstone** — biggest, most dramatic upgrade of the run |

> At levels 2, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10 the game pauses and shows **3 cards** on screen (Dead Cells / Hades style). The player picks one. Each card meaningfully changes combat, not just bumps numbers.

### Upgrade Paths (Locked at L7)

At level 7 the player chooses one path. This determines what cards appear at L8 and L10.

| Path | Identity | L8 Cards | L10 Capstone |
|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| ⚔️ **Power** | Hit harder, combos longer | Damage amp, combo extension, execute threshold | All status effects deal 50% more damage for rest of run |
| 🛡 **Survival** | Stay alive, heal through combat | Shield rune, lifesteal on kills, second wind | Once per run: survive a lethal hit at 1 HP then recover 40 HP |
| 💨 **Mobility** | Move faster, dodge better | Extra dodge charge, dash cooldown cut, dodge i-frames +4 | Dodge becomes briefly invincible and deals damage on exit |
| ✨ **Style** | Aura escalation, spell synergies, silent casting | Silent casting (no mana sound cue), aura blazes, spell weave | Casting a spell within 1s of a combo hit always crits |

---

### Passives Available at L5

When the L5 card choice offers a passive, the 3 options are drawn from this pool (weighted by hunter identity):

| Passive | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| *Bleed Hunger* | Killing a bleeding enemy restores 5 HP (Dabik-weighted) |
| *Iron Resolve* | Take 15% less damage when below 40% HP (Benzu-weighted) |
| *Speed Read* | After a perfect dodge, next attack deals +30% damage (Sereisa-weighted) |
| *Mana Loop* | Killing an enemy with a spell reduces next spell cost by 50% (Vesol-weighted) |
| *Combo Memory* | Combo counter doesn't reset on a miss once per zone (all) |
| *Essence Surge* | Boss kills drop +30% Essence (all) |

## Part 2 — The Shop

### Structure

- Opens in the **Hub between every zone**
- Shows **5 random items** from the pool
- **Reroll** costs 30 Essence (or use a Reroll Token)
- **Max 2 purchases per visit** — prevents snowballing
- One random **cosmetic** is always free on zone clear

### Two Currencies, Distinct Feelings

| | Essence 🟠 | XP ⚡ |
|--|------------|-------|
| Source | Enemy drops, boss drops, zone clear bonus | Earned automatically by playing well |
| Spent at | Hub shop | Auto — triggers level-up on threshold |
| Wipe behaviour | Keep 50% | Full reset |
| Feel | Economic decisions | Natural growth |

---

### Essence Drop Rates

| Source | Essence |
|--------|---------|
| Grunt kill | 10-20 |
| Ranged unit kill | 15-25 |
| Bruiser kill | 30-50 |
| Boss kill | 200 |
| Zone clear bonus | 100 |
| No-damage wave bonus | +50 |

> **Full clean run estimate:** the removed gate reward is now distributed across the longer wave budget and boss clear rewards. Retune the wave tables if the economy feels too generous or too tight in playtests.
> At max 2 buys per visit x 4 visits = **8 shop purchases** over a run. This keeps the shop meaningful - you cannot buy everything.

### Shop × Level Synergy

The shop adapts to what you've unlocked:

| Run stage | What the shop emphasises |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| After Zone 1 (L1–3) | Stat cards, potions, mobility — survivability basics |
| After Zone 2 (L4–6) | Spell items start appearing (you have Advanced spell now) |
| After Zone 3 (L7–8) | Path-aligned items surface more (Power items if Power path, etc.) |
| After Zone 4 (L9–10) | If you survived — cosmetics + ultra items for next run motivation |

---

## Summary Flow

```
Zone 1 clear
  → L3: Advanced spell unlocked
  → Shop: basics + first spell item available

Zone 2 clear
  → L4–5: Minor spell modified + passive choice
  → Shop: spell items appear, mobility/power options

Zone 3 clear
  → L6–7: Advanced spell modified + path lock
  → Shop: path-weighted items surface

Zone 4 boss kill
  → L9–10: Ultimate unlocked + capstone
  → Run complete — build fully realised
```
