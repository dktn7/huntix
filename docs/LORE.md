## The World

The gates appeared without warning. Spatial rifts — permanent, unmappable, hostile — tearing open in cities, wilderness, underground. Inside each gate: a contained dimension seething with monsters.

Governments collapsed trying to close them. Then hunters emerged — individuals with anomalous combat ability, able to enter gates and fight back. Ranked by power output, organised into guilds, dispatched into gates before the creatures can breach into the real world.

Most hunters never reach S-Rank. Those who do are effectively forces of nature. Four of them are in this story.

---

## Gate Mechanics (World Rules)

- Gates are **one-way entry** — once you step through, the gate locks behind you until cleared
- A gate is **cleared** when the apex creature inside is killed
- **Uncleared gates** eventually breach — creatures flood into the real world
- Gates have **elemental affinities** — fire, earth, void, lightning — which determine creature types inside
- **S-Rank gates** (the ones in this game) have never been successfully cleared until now

## 🔥 Vesol — The Ember Mage

**"The gate burns."

### Background
Vesol grew up in a research facility that studied gate phenomena — the one with no name on any public record. By the time she was sixteen, she had manifested flame-class abilities so intense the facility classified her as a containment risk rather than an asset.

She left. The facility burned. She maintains it was an accident.

By twenty-two she was S-Rank, notorious for leaving gates as smouldering voids. Her method: enter, ignite everything, walk out. She has never failed a gate clear. She has never explained how she survives her own Inferno.

### Personality
Controlled. Dry. Speaks in short sentences. Dislikes wasted motion. Her stillness before a fight is the most dangerous thing about her — it means she has already decided how it ends.

### The Gate That Changed Her
A fire-affinity S-Rank gate appeared over a coastal city. Every A-Rank sent in did not come back. Vesol entered alone. Inside she found something that could not be burned — something that looked back at her and smiled. She killed it anyway. She has not spoken about what it was.

### Powers
- **Element:** Flame / Burn
- **Weapon:** Wrist Crystal Focus (channels mana into directed flame)
- **Signature:** Flame walls, sustained burn stacks, area denial
- **Ultimate:** Inferno — entire arena ignites for 6 seconds, Vesol immune to own flames

## 🌑 Dabik — The Shadow Striker

**"Silence is the last thing you hear."

### Background
Dabik's rank was disputed for three years. His combat ability registered as A-Rank on every standard test. The committee argued he didn't qualify for S-Rank because none of his kills were witnessed — no body cameras survived his gate clears, no surviving creatures reported his attacks, no other hunters who entered alongside him could describe what they saw.

He was reclassified S-Rank after a joint operation in which twelve A-Rank hunters entered a void-class gate and Dabik was the only one to come out. The other eleven were fine. They just couldn't explain what had happened.

### Personality
Quiet. Economical. Finds most conversation unnecessary. Gets along best with Sereisa (she talks enough for both of them). Harbours a specific, personal vendetta against KIBAD — the rogue angel inside the Shadow Core gate — that he has never explained.

### The Gate That Changed Him
Dabik was the first hunter to enter a void-class gate. He was seventeen. He was supposed to be an observer. He came out alone, three days later, carrying the gate core. No one knows what happened inside. He doesn't say. The void-class gates have not been assigned to any other hunter since.

### Powers
- **Element:** Shadow / Bleed
- **Weapon:** Twin Curved Daggers
- **Signature:** Blink teleport, bleed stacks, backstab multiplier
- **Ultimate:** Monarch's Domain — all enemies frozen 4s, Dabik invisible rapid-strike mode

## 🔴 Benzu — The Iron Breaker

**"I don't dodge."

### Background
Benzu came from the underground — literally. He grew up in the deep sub-levels of a collapsed urban zone where a gate had opened thirty years earlier and never been cleared. The creatures inside had adapted. So had he.

When the Hunter Association finally mounted a serious operation on the gate, they found Benzu already inside, living in a fortified position he had built from creature bones and gate-fused stone. He had been surviving solo for eleven years. He was twenty-three.

He agreed to come out, provided he could go back in when he wanted.

### Personality
Blunt. Literal. Has no patience for strategy meetings but executes perfectly in the field. The other hunters trust him unconditionally in combat. Outside of combat, he is approximately impossible to manage. He considers ZARTH — the earth-class boss of Ruin Den — a personal nemesis, as it was the first creature to successfully stagger him.

### The Gate That Changed Him
The exit of the Ruin Den gate didn't exist when Benzu fell in. He was twelve. He survived because he refused to accept the alternative. When he finally emerged eleven years later, the first thing he said was: "The exit didn't exist until I made one."

### Powers
- **Element:** Thunder / Earth / Stun
- **Weapon:** Stone-Forged Gauntlets (gate-fused material, nearly indestructible)
- **Signature:** Shoulder charge, ground slam, high stagger on heavy hits
- **Ultimate:** Titan's Wrath — full-screen ground shatter, all enemies stunned 5s, Benzu zero damage

## ⚡ Sereisa — The Storm Chaser

**"You blinked."

### Background
Sereisa was ranked S before she was eighteen, which made her the youngest S-Rank in recorded history and the subject of approximately three hundred articles she has never read. She was already bored by the time they gave her the rank.

She comes from a long line of A-Rank hunters — lightning-class abilities run in the family, but hers manifested at a scale no one expected. Her mother, a respected A-Rank, described watching Sereisa's first combat assessment as "watching someone explain that the speed limit doesn't apply to them."

She treats gate clears like a personal time trial. She holds the record for fastest S-Rank gate clear: eleven minutes, forty seconds. She is trying to break ten.

### Personality
Energetic. Verbally relentless. Finds standing still physically uncomfortable. Gets along best with Dabik (she appreciates that he listens without pretending to keep up). Has a genuine warmth underneath the speed — she is the one who notices when the others are struggling and says something, quickly, and then moves on.

### The Gate That Changed Her
The Thunder Spire gate opened during a storm, which Sereisa considered a good sign. Inside, she found THYXIS — a creature built entirely from condensed gate-lightning, faster than her, and the first opponent to ever make her run. She escaped. She came back. The second time, she was ready. She describes the fight as "the first time I had to actually try."

### Powers
- **Element:** Lightning / Slow
- **Weapon:** Twin Electro-Blades
- **Signature:** Electric dash, lightning chains between enemies, speed aura
- **Ultimate:** Storm Surge — untouchable 6s, every dash deals damage, speed doubles

## The Gates in This Run

### 🏙 City Breach — VRAEL
An industrial-research district gate. The facility inside has no name on any public record — the same one Vesol grew up in. What she finds inside when she returns is not what she expected.

**VRAEL** is a fire bruiser evolved from the facility's own containment failures. It did not exist before Vesol left. The implication is not subtle.

### 🏕 Ruin Den — ZARTH
The gate Benzu spent eleven years inside. The exit didn't exist when he fell in — he made one. ZARTH is the earth-class apex creature, an entity of compressed stone and gate-fused earth. It was there the whole time. Benzu and ZARTH have been aware of each other for eleven years.

This is the rematch.

### 🌑 Shadow Core — KIBAD
A void-class gate — the kind only Dabik has ever cleared. KIBAD is a rogue angel, something that should not exist in a gate context. It ascends smiling when defeated. Dabik knows why. He hasn't said.

### ⚡ Thunder Spire — THYXIS
Sereisa's gate. The creature that made her run. THYXIS is the first opponent she couldn't immediately overwhelm. A gate-born thunder beast, it embodies storm energy at a scale that rivals Sereisa's own output. Defeating it, the storm stops. The wolf inside it walks out. That part isn't in the briefing.
